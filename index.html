Filipino Culture Corner

Word of the Day

"Salamat"

/sa-la-mat/

Meaning "Thank you". Filipinos value gratitude and saying "Salamat" with a smile goes a long way!

Cultural Tip

When greeting elders, it's respectful to say "Mano po" while taking their hand and gently pressing it to your forehead. This shows respect in Filipino culture.

Did You Know?

Tagalog is the basis of the Filipino language, but there are actually over 170 languages spoken across the Philippines!
